Tempe

Tempe is a vibrant city in the East Valley of Arizona, home to Arizona State University. With a population of 195,805 people as of 2025, Tempe was incorporated in 1894. The city is located in the central part of Arizona and is classified as an urban area. According to recent data from World Population Review, Tempe has a median household income of $71,406, which is competitive within the state.

Known for its college town atmosphere, Tempe combines academic energy with desert beauty. The city offers numerous outdoor activities along Tempe Town Lake and is famous for its vibrant nightlife scene around Mill Avenue.
