Glendale

The city of Glendale is a vibrant and welcoming metropolitan city located in the northwest part of Arizona. Incorporated in 1910, Glendale is home to 257,125 people as of 2025. This urban city has a household income of $89,767 according to World Population Review, which is above the state average.

Glendale is known for its sports venues, including State Farm Stadium (home of the Arizona Cardinals) and Gila River Arena. The city offers a mix of suburban and urban living with excellent shopping and dining options.
